Uncharted - A Virtual Fashion Show hosted by the Fashion and Design Society
FADS is a student-run organization that supports the talented fashion design students of UC Davis. We have taken our annual fashion show online this year to showcase the dynamic collections created by students of DES 179 “Signature Collection” class. Join us to explore the “Uncharted” territories that fashion can reach!
We are accepting submissions of single garments to be shown during the live premiere of the fashion show in Spring Quarter. These can be assignments from class or your own personal projects. Each person may submit only one piece. After the deadline, our team will contact you if you are selected.
Deadline: Friday, March 19 - single garment submission

In the meantime, join a committee if you'd like to help out!
