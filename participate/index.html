Become a FADS Member!

2021-2022 Member deadline Oct 14
Although most of our events will continue to be open to the public, if you want to participate more you can become a FADS member! By participating in FADS, students not only gain a close community of fellow designers, they further the growth of fashion at UC Davis and learn vital leadership and technical skills for their future careers.
As a FADS member, you:
  • get access to member only events each quarter (socials, networking events, special workshops, etc)
  • have higher potential of being selected for leadership positions
  • can only miss 2 general meetings per quarter
